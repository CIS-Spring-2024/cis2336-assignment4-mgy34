About Cafe EASE

Cafe EASE was created by Madison Young, intending to create a safe spot for students to reside and spend time away from the busy school life. With the ability to spend time in a quiet but comfortable environment, Cafe EASE allows for students to snack and sip away their worries in the cafe's menu of a variety of items. Additionally, there is a lot of sitting space allowing students to spend time together without bumping into another student.


To visit Cafe EASE, no reservation is necessary. All you need to do is walk-in and place an order. Additional information can be found on their respective pages around the website. We hope to see you there!
