Frequently Asked Questions

Please use this page to answer any possible questions you have! If you have a question that hasn't been answered, please refer to our contact page for additional assistance.

Where is Cafe EASE?

Cafe EASE is situated at the University of Houston's Sugar Land campus next to the library.

Do you need a reservation?

You do not need a reservation. Feel free to walk in and order. Seating may be limited during busy hours.

What does Cafe EASE sell?

Cafe EASE's menu can be found here! For special orders, please check out the form on that page or order at the counter.

Can I bring pets into Cafe EASE?

Only trained service animals are allowed inside Cafe EASE due to the presence of food and the promise of a relaxing environment.

Is Cafe EASE accessible?

Cafe EASE makes a promise to have accessible entrances and bathrooms available. Special seating arrangements can also be made if necessary.
